# Pan African Science Hub (PASH) - Admin Guide

This guide explains how to manage the website's content, including adding new articles, deleting old ones, and reading messages sent by visitors through the Contact form.

## 1. Accessing the Admin Dashboard
1. Open your web browser and go to your website's admin URL: **`yourwebsite.com/admin`**
2. You will be prompted to enter the **Admin Password**. *(Please get this password from the site administrator).*
3. Once you enter the correct password, you will be logged into the dashboard.

---

## 2. Navigating the Dashboard
At the top of the admin dashboard, you will see two main tabs:
- **Articles:** This tab allows you to view, add, edit, and delete news articles or blog posts.
- **Messages:** This tab allows you to read messages sent by users from the "Contact Us" section on the main website.

---

## 3. Managing Articles (The "Articles" Tab)

### How to Add a New Article
1. Click on the **Articles** tab at the top.
2. Click the **"+ New Article"** button.
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Title:** The headline of your article.
   - **Category:** Select a category (e.g., Innovation, Research, Outreach) or type a new one.
   - **Cover Image URL:** Paste a link to an image (e.g., from an image hosting site or Google Drive link). This will be the main picture for the article.
   - **Summary:** A short, 1-2 sentence description of the article.
   - **Content:** The full text of your article. You can use Markdown to format it (e.g., `**bold**`, `# Heading`).
4. Click **"Save Article"** at the bottom. Your article is now live on the website!

### How to Edit an Existing Article
1. Scroll through the list of articles to find the one you want to change.
2. Click the **"Edit"** button next to that article.
3. The form will open with the article's current details. Make your changes.
4. Click **"Save Article"**.

### How to Delete an Article
1. Find the article you want to remove from the list.
2. Click the red **"Delete"** button next to it.
3. **Warning:** Deleting an article is permanent. Once deleted, it will immediately disappear from the public website.

---

## 4. Reading Messages (The "Messages" Tab)

Whenever a visitor fills out the Contact form on the main website, their message is saved securely.

### How to View Messages
1. Click on the **Messages** tab at the top of the admin dashboard.
2. You will see a list of all messages, sorted with the newest ones at the top.
3. Each message card will display:
   - **Name:** The person who sent the message.
   - **Email:** Their contact email address (so you can reply to them from your own email account).
   - **Date:** Exactly when the message was sent.
   - **Message:** What they wrote.

*Note: You cannot reply directly from this dashboard. To reply, copy the person's email address and send them an email from your official PASH email account.*

---

## 5. Security Best Practices
- **Do not share the admin password** with anyone outside of the core team.
- Always review your articles for typos before clicking "Save Article".
- If the dashboard ever fails to load, simply refresh the page.
